1. Vision statement and objectives

1.1 Our vision

xx

1.2 Objectives

  • Cement the UK’s position as the best place in Europe to start and grow a business by supporting local growth, entrepreneurs, and making it easier for small businesses to resolve disputes quickly and easily
  • Deliver 3 million new apprenticeships to ensure businesses have access to the skilled workforce they need, while also taking measures to improve further education
  • Support competitiveness by cutting red tape, generating new trade, investment and job opportunities, opening up the Single Market, and developing our long-term industrial strategy
  • Tackle illegal practices in the workplace, legislate to reform trade union strike action, and deal with the abuse of the minimum wage
  • Ensure that the UK is the best place to innovate, maintain a world leading research and science base to drive growth and productivity while reforming the system to maximise value from our investments
  • Ensure a sustainable, autonomous English higher education system with world-class teaching that is open to people from all backgrounds

3.1 What BIS is doing

BIS is at the centre of the government’s drive to create 3 million apprenticeships and join up initiatives across government. We’re also introducing a range of new measures including a new levy of large employers for apprenticeship starts and continuing to improve the further education sector.
